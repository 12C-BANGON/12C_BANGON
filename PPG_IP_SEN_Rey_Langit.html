    • Reynante "Rey" Langit

    Running for Senator, Independent


    Researched by Moira Ibarrola



    Education


    1. Finished his primary education at the F.G. Caldreon Elementary School, a public school in Manila.


    2. First year of High School in Manuel L. Quezon University


    3. Finished the rest of his high school education at the Far Eastern University Boys High School


    4. Pursued his college education at the Far Eastern University


    5. He finished second year law at the FEU College of Law





    Accomplishments


    1. He is a BS Commerce graduate and an exemplary student leader.


    2. Director of the Order of Parliamentarians Confraternity


    3. President of the IABF Banking Association


    4. A Model Cadet and ROTC Officer


    5. President of FEU Philippine Cultural Performance Arts Group


    6. Director of the FEU Progressive Party


    7. Joined the media, becoming a radio announcer, anchorman, and commentator


    8. Awarded the prestigious "Radio Broadcast Journalist of the Year" award given by the Rotary Club of Manila the following year


    9. Named "Radio Broadcast Journalist of the Year" by the Rotary Club of Manila for his outstanding achievements in public service through broadcast media and his exceptional and in- depth reports on relevant and important issues (2004)


    10. Was the Station Manager of the AM Radio station DWIZ 882:kHz


    11. Vice president of Aliw Broadcasting Corporation


    12. Chairman of the Kapisanan ng mga Brodkaster ng Pilipinas-Metro Manila Chapter


    13. A member of the board of directors of the KBP-National


    14. The Chairman of the Heavenly Images Productions for Television, Inc. (HIP TV, Inc.) which is an independent media production house producing its own television and radio programs.


    15. Langit ran for senator thrice in the 1998, 2010, and 2016 national elections under the Partido ng Demokratikong Reporma–Lapiang Manggagawa (Reporma–LM), the Lakas Kampi–CMD, and the United Nationalist Alliance but lost.





    Advocacies


    1. Manila Speak


    2. UP Cebu Forum that promotes peace end communist conflict


    3. Joins 8 Trimedia Broadcasting Network


    4. Filmography
