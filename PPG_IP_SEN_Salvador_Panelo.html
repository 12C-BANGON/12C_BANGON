    • Salvador “Sal” Panelo

    Running for Senator, Partido Demokratiko Pilipino–Lakas ng Bayan


    Researched by Arin Lee



    Education


    1. BS Political Science and BA English degrees

    - University of the Philippines – Diliman


    2. Completed law school (1974)

    - University of the Philippines College of Law





    Accomplishments


    Private lawyer

    - Lawyered Dennis Roldan for the kidnapping a Filipino-Chinese boy in 2005

    - Lawyered for the family of Benjamin Abalos, former chairman of the National Election Commission, Philip Medel, convicted of the murder of Nida Blanca, and murdered racing champion Enzo Pastor in 2007

    - Lawyered Datu Unsay Mayor Andal Ampatuan Jr. for his involvement in the 2009 Maguindanao massacr





    Advocacies


    1. On May 1, 2020, the People's Television Network Talk Show began, inciting certain critics and others who oppose the government's specific policies.


    2. Argued that lawyers do not lie and showed belief in his own behavior during the interview.





    Corruption Cases


    No sources were found on the corruption cases filed against Salvador “Sal” Panelo.





    Scandals


    He criticized the virus meme on social media for his presumptive remarks on national and local elections in 2022. (2021)





    Family Background


    1. Salvador Panelo is from Naga, Camarines Sur.
