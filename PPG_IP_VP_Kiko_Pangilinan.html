    • Francis "Kiko" Pangilingan

    Running for Vice President, Liberal Party


    Researched by Nadine Roque



    Education


    1. Primary and Secondary Education, La Salle Greenhills


    2. Bachelor of Arts in English, Major in Comparative Literature, University of the Philippines Diliman


    2. Bachelor of Laws, University of the Philippines Diliman


    2. Masters in Public Administration, John F. Kennedy School of Government Harvard University

    - Area of Concentration: Strategic Management





    Accomplishments


    1. Senator, Republic of the Philippines, 2001 – 2013


    2. Officer of the Senate Majority Leader, Senate of the Philippines, 2004 – 2008


    3. Cabinet Secretary. Presidential Assistant for Food Security and Agricultural Modernization Office of the President, May 2014 – September 2015


    4. Senator, Republic of the Philippines, June 2016 – Present

    - August 2, 2017

    Principal Author and Co-sponsor of the Republic Act 10929 (Free Internet Access in Public Places Law); An act establishing the free internet access program in public places in the country and appropriating funds therefor

    - August 3, 2017

    Co-author of the Republic Act 10931 (Universal Access to Quality Tertiary Education Act); An act promoting universal access to quality tertiary education by providing for free tuition and other school fees in state universities and colleges, local universities and colleges and state-run technical-vocational institutions, establishing the tertiary education subsidy and student loan program, strengthening the unified student financial assistance system for tertiary education, and appropriating fund therefor

    - February 20, 2019

    Principal Author and Co-sponsor of the Republic Act 11210 (105-day Expanded Maternity Leave Law); An act increasing the maternity leave period to one hundred five (105) days for female workers with an option to extend for an additional thirty (30) days without pay, and granting an additional fifteen (15) days for solo mothers, and for other purposes

    - April 17, 2019

    Principal Author and Sponsor of the Republic Act 11321 (Sagip Saka Act); An act instituting the farmers and fisherfolk enterprise development program of the department of agriculture

    - February 26, 2021

    Co-author of the Republic Act No. 11525 (COVID-19 Vaccination Program Act of 2021); An act establishing the Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) vaccination program expediting the vaccine procurement and administration process, providing funds therefor, and for other purposes

    - March 13, 2021

    Principal Author of the Republic Act 11524 (Coconut Farmers and Industry Trust Fund Act); An act creating the coconut farmers and industry trust fund, providing for its management and utilization, reconstituting for the purpose the Philippine coconut authority board, and for other purposes


    5. Minority Leader, Commission on Appointments, May 2017 – June 2020


    6. Member of Senate Electoral Tribunal, July 2020 - Present





    Advocacies


    1. Sagip Saka (2019 - Present)


    2. Public Assistance Program (Present)


    3. Tablet Operations (Present)





    Corruption Cases


    No sources were found on the corruption cases filed against Kiko Pangilingan.





    Scandals


    1. Pangilinan denied rumors of him having an illegitimate child.


    2. Pangilinan was said to be involved in Priority Development Assistance Fund (PDAF) scam last 2013. His wife, Sharon Cuneta-Pangilinan, defended him through social media, saying that she is confident her husband did not steal anything.


    3. Pangilinan sued YouTube for hosting fake and malicious videos claiming domestic violence and adultery in his family.





    Family Background


    1. His wife is Sharon Cuneta-Pangilinan.


    2. His children are Ma. Kristina Cassandra (stepdaughter), Simone Francesca Emmanuelle, Mariel Daniella Sophia, and Miguel Samuel Mateo (adopted)
