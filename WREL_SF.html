    • The subjects Introduction to World Religions and Belief Systems (IWRBS) and English for Academic and Professional Purposes (EAPP), challenged students from the Humanities Strand to create feature articles centered on the role of religion in current social and political issues. The goal of this activity was to expose the students to a new style of writing and apply the concepts they have acquired from IWRBS to critically analyze Religion in Society. The students tackled subjects such as radicalism, block voting, and extrajudicial killings, very timely discussions with the nearing 2022 Philippine elections.

    ABOUT BANGON

    BANGON is a website created by 12C of PAREF Woodrose School. BANGON means arise or to get up. The class aims to arise, raise our voices and showcase our perspectives in the different sectors and issues the Philippine society faces today through creative and literary outputs.

    Ang "BANGON" ay isang website na inilikha ng 12C ng PAREF Woodrose School. Ang pagtindig o pagtayo mula sa upuan o sa pagkahiga mula sa kama ang kahulugan ng bangon. Nais ipaalam ng klase ang kanilang paninindigan tungkol sa iba't ibang bahagi at suliranin ng lipunan sa pamamagitan ng malikhaing pagsulat at sining.

    AHON

    AHON is a campaign led by 12C of PAREF Woodrose School that gives awareness on fake news and voter's education.
