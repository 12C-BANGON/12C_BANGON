    • Arnel Paciano Casanova

    Running for Senator, KAMPI


    Researched by Megan Sison



    Education


    1. Harvard University Cambridge, MA, USA John F. Kennedy School of Government

    - Masters in Public Administration (Finance and Leadership), June 2007 Fellow, Edward S. Mason Program in Public Policy and Management World Bank Scholar


    2. Certificate on Negotiations of Financial Transactions (online) Geneva, Switzerland, May-June 2006

    - United Nations Institure for Training and Research


    3. University of the Philippines Diliman

    - Bachelor of Laws (1998)

    - School of Urban and Regional Planning, Diploma on Urban and Regional Planning (2001)

    - College of Arts and Letters, Bachelor of Arts in English (1992)





    Accomplishments


    1. The former president and CEO of BCDA (Bases Conversion Development Authority)


    2. Country Representative for AECOM (architecture, engineering, construction, operations, and management)


    3. The founder of Clark Green City


    4. Philippine Legion of Honor Medal (1997)


    5. Responsible for the SCTEX and BGC (Bonifacio Global City) as they were BCDA projects when he was CEO





    Advocacies


    1. He implemented governance reforms in government


    2. Corporations and won a case against Philippine armed forces generals to recover illegally-acquired government property worth $200 million.


    3. He drafted peace agreements with the military rebels and the Muslim rebels in the Philippines


    4. He teaches social entrepreneurship in Ateneo De Manila University, Philippines


    5. He is responsible for a number of major property development projects and public-private partnerships that have established advanced and modern economic districts.





    Corruption Cases


    Was accused of involvement for libel against Robert John Sobrepeña in 2017, the Chairman of Camp John Hay Development Corp. He was then bailed at Php 10,000 set by the Pasig Trial Court.





    Scandals


    No sources were found on scandals involving Arnel Paciano Casanova.





    Family Background


    1. Lived in a poor family in Padre Garcia, Batangas

    2. Had 7 siblings

    3. Father is a farmer and mother is a seamstress
