    • Mark Aguilar Villar

    Running for Senator, Nationalista Party


    Researched by Ia Barrientos



    Education


    1. Bachelor's Degree in Economics, Political Science and Philosophy

    - Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania


    2. Master's Degree in Business Administration University of Chicago

    - Booth School of Business





    Accomplishments


    1. IATF-EID Isolation Czar (2020-2021)

    - We Heal As One Center, 819 quarantine facilities with 29,776 beds were put up throughout 2020-2021 and are operational today.


    2. Secretary of Public Works and Highways (2016-2021)

    - Supervised the completion of 32,527 km of roads, 8,496 national bridges, 150,149 classrooms, 230 evacuation centers, 11,340 flood control projects across the country and 755 We Heal As One quarantine facilities

    - Disclaimer: these projects are a combination of carried-over infrastructure projects from the PNoy administration and Duterte administration.


    3. Congressman of the Lone District; concurrently served as the Chairman of the House Committee on Trade and Industry and Vice Chairman of the House Committees on Overseas Workers Affairs, Labor and Employment, and Science and Technology (2010-2016)

    - Authored Republic Act No. 10644 otherwise known as the Go Negosyo Act seeks to promote micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSME’s) to produce more job opportunities. It was signed into law on July 15, 2014 by the former President Benigno S. Aquino III

    - Led the passage of Republic Act No. 10668 or An Act Allowing Forgeign Vessels to Transport and Co-Load Foreign Cargoes for Domestic Transshipment and other Purposes signed into law on July 21, 2015 by the former President Benigno S. Aquino III

    - Co-authored Republic Act No. 10642 or Philippine Lemon Law passed in 2014 “to promote the full protection of the rights of consumers against business and trade practices in the sale of motor vehicles which are deceptive, unfair or otherwise inimical to consumers and the public” (Section 2, Republic Act No.10642)

    - In September of 2013 he filed, An Act Establishing A Student Financial Assistance Program (StuFAP) For Students Of Economically Disadvantaged Families

    - Authored 76 bills and 20 resolutions

    - Co-authored 75 bills





    Advocacies


    1. Alternative Mobility


    2. Last August Bigay Trabaho, an online job fair, was launched. It aims to employ 1.6 million more workers to various job contractors in the Philippines

    No sources were found on the corruption cases filed against Mark Villar.





    Scandals


    On September 3, 2021, Mark Villar posted a photo of the Iloilo Esplanade with the caption “#BuildBuildBuild” on his verified facebook page. This post outraged many Filipino netizens, many of which were Iloilo residents, since the esplanade began its construction in past administrations. The netizens accused him of credit-grabbing and made satirical memes of his post.
