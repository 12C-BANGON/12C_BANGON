    • Nur-Ana Sahidullah

    Running for Senator, Katipunan ng Demokratikong Pilipino


    Researched by Annika Guce



    Education


    1. Reohondo Elementary School


    2. Southern City Colleges High School in Zamboanga


    3. Received her Bachelor of Science in Commerce from Zamboanga AE Colleges in 1980


    4. Also received a Bachelor of Arts in Mass Communication in 1988 from Western Mindanao State University, Zamboanga City





    Accomplishments


    1. Was the mayor of Banguingui Sulu for two consecutive terms (1998–2001, 2001–04)


    2. Served two terms as vice-governor of Sulu (2004–07, 2007–10)


    3. Congresswoman, Sulu 2nd District (2010-2013)


    4. Ran for a Senate post in 2019, but lost.


    5. Senator 2004-2014


    6. In 1995 she established the Anak Ilo Foundation


    7. Has been chairwoman of the Philippine National Red Cross, Sulu Chapter since 2004 (has a reputation as an advocate of peace and justice and is a negotiator)





    Advocacies


    1. Wage Increase


    2. Disaster Risk Reduction and Mitigation


    3. She and her husband (former Bangungui mayor Abulwahid Omar Sahidulla) were sentenced to one year in prison by the anti-graft court Sandiganbayan in February 2019 for failing to declare their property in their joint statement of assets, liabilities, and net worth (SALN)


    4. If elected will prioritize the generation of businesses and jobs amid the COVID-19 pandemic.
