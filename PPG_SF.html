    Good Governance and Multi-Sectoral Synergy for Pandemic Response in Western Visayas, Philippines

    As part of an in-depth look at the West Philippine Sea conflict with China, students of the General Academic and Humanities and Social Sciences strands within the subject Philippine Politics and Governance demonstrated the weight of this issue through artistic means. The students were tasked to create a poster depicting the struggle in asserting the sovereign rights of the country over its Exclusive Economic Zone (EEZ). They were given the liberty to design their poster however they saw fit, implementing the forestanding ideals and rights that the Philippines has over its marine resources within its EEZ. A clear understanding of this issue will help new voters consider the political candidates whose advocacy is to assert and fight for the sovereign rights of the Philippines in the West Philippine Sea.

    With the upcoming national elections happening in 2022, It is important that voters should be made aware and educated on the candidates’ political experiences and important accomplishments. As a result, the students of the General Academics and Humanities and Social Sciences strand were tasked to research and gather the necessary information for their assigned candidate as part of their Philippine Politics and Governance class. This task will benefit the students and voters to get to know more about their future leaders so they have a basis on which candidate they will cast their vote for.

    ABOUT BANGON

    BANGON is a website created by 12C of PAREF Woodrose School. BANGON means arise or to get up. The class aims to arise, raise our voices and showcase our perspectives in the different sectors and issues the Philippine society faces today through creative and literary outputs.

    Ang "BANGON" ay isang website na inilikha ng 12C ng PAREF Woodrose School. Ang pagtindig o pagtayo mula sa upuan o sa pagkahiga mula sa kama ang kahulugan ng bangon. Nais ipaalam ng klase ang kanilang paninindigan tungkol sa iba't ibang bahagi at suliranin ng lipunan sa pamamagitan ng malikhaing pagsulat at sining.
