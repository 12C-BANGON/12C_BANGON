    Education


    1. Bachelor of Laws

    - University of the Philippines


    2. Fellow of Harvard Kennedy School on Developmental Leadership


    3. Master in Business Administration (MBA) degree Senior Executive Program

    - University of the East (UE)


    4. Doctorate degree (Ph.D.) in Public Administration and Governance (2020)

    - at the University of the Philippines National College of Public Administration and Governance (UP-NCPAG)





    Accomplishments


    1. Congressman

    - as nominee of Ang Alagad Partylist (2004-2013)

    - as the nominee of SAGIP Partylist (2016-present)


    2. Deputy Speaker of the House of Representatives (2019-present)


    3. Bills/laws/resolutions proposed:

    - House Bill 144 – An act providing for the establishment of a national framework for mandatory environmental insurance coverage for certain environmentally critical projects

    - House bill 145 – an act defining political dynasty and prohibiting the establishment thereof

    - House bill 146 – an act providing for a prescriptive period from which COA may conduct audits and issue a decision on cases, amending the state audit code of the Philippines (p.d. 1445) for such purposes

    - House bill 147 – an act excluding the services performed by employees of religious entities from employment services covered by social security system law, amending for the purpose republic act no. 8282 otherwise known as the ꞌsocial security act of 1997ꞌ

    - House bill 149 – an act increasing the total indemnity claim for death or serious physical injury to any passenger or third party without the necessity of proving fault or negligence, amending for the purpose section 378 of the insurance code of the Philippines

    - House bill 150 – an act establishing the overseas Filipino worker’s social security and retirement system and appropriating funds therefor

    - House Resolution 1058 – A Resolution to Inquire in Aid of Legislation on the Authenticity of the Land Title of ABS-CBN


    4. Hosts a morning TV/radio public affairs talk show, “Sa Ganang Mamamayan” aired over Net 25 and Radyo Agila DZEC-AM 1062





    Advocacies


    Provided pro bono legal services to the poor and vulnerable, particularly to the victims of unlawful eviction and demolition cases (2014-2016)
