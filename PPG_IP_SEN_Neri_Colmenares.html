    • Neri Javier Colmenares

    Running for Senate, Makabayan


    Researched by Camille Duran



    Education


    1. University of Melbourne, PhD (2002)


    2. Graduate: University of the Philippines, College of Law (1996)


    3. Tertiary: San Beda College, Bachelor of Arts in Economics (1992)


    4. Secondary: St. Joseph College


    5. Primary: La Consolacion College






    Accomplishments


    1. In the 2020 International Bar Association, Neri Colmenares was awarded with the award for Outstanding Contribution to Human Rights.


    2. Neri Colmenares is a renowned human rights lawyer. He has handled high-profile cases, such as the following: the impeachment cases of Renato Corona, Ombudsman Merci Gutierrez, and President Gloria Arroyo, constitutionality of Executive Order 464, Emergency Rule Proclamation 1017, and the Visiting Forces Agreement in the light of the Subic Rape Case.


    3. As a congressman, Colmenares was able to author and co-author numerous laws, such as the following:

    - Republic Act No. 9745 – Anti-Torture Act

    - Republic Act No. 10069 – Health Workers’ Day Act

    - Republic Act No. 10353 – Anti-Enforced or Involuntary Disappearance Act

    - Republic Act No. 10366 – Accessible Polling Places for Persons with Disabilities and Senior Citizens

    - Republic Act No. 10368 – Human Rights Victims Reparation and Recognition Act

    - Republic Act No. 10380 - Local Absentee Voting for Media

    - Republic Act No. 10609 - Protection of Students’ Right to Enroll in Review Centers Act

    - Republic Act No. 10639 – Free Mobile Disaster Act

    - Republic Act No. 10653- Increased Ceiling for Tax Exempt Benefits

    - Republic Act No. 10756 - Election Service Reform ACT (ESRA)


    4. From 1996 till present times, Colmenares was able to build his work experience and political career.

    - Chairman, National Union of Peoples’ Lawyers (NUPL)

    - Ran for senator, 2019 (Lost)

    - Ran for senator, 2016 (Lost)

    - House of Representatives, Congressman Bayan Muna Partylist, 01 July 2013 ¬ 2016

    - House of Representatives, Congressman Bayan Muna Parylist, 01 July 2010 ¬ 30 June 2013

    - Office of Representative Satur Ocampo, Consultant, 01 March 2004 ¬ 30 March 2006

    - Bayan Muna Partylist, General Counsel, 19 September 1999 ¬ 2009

    - National Amnesty Commission, Legal Officer November 1996- September 1999

    - Pro Bono Lawyer for Farmers, Workers and Urban Poor 1996-2009






    Advocacies
